Chicken Alfredo

Description

This easy chicken alfredo can be made inabout twenty minutes without breaking the bank for comfort foods

Ingredients

  • Frozen Breaded chicken of Choice
  • Alfredo sauce of choice
  • Pasta of choice
  • Shredded cheese of choice

Steps

  1. Put your chicken into an air fryer at 425 for 15 minutes, put your pasta into a pan of boiling water for about 10-15 minutes or until soft
  2. After noodles have soften, drain the water from the noodles, get a pyrex pan to transfer noodles into
  3. After putting noodles into pyrex pan pour alfredo sauce ontop and then place chicken ontop of the sauce
  4. Then top with cheese and put into oven at 425 for about 10-15 minutes or until golden brown
