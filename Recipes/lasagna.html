Lasagna

Description

This is a lazy version of lasagna with minimal ingredients but a lot of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Itailian Seasoning
  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  • Brown up hamburger meat on stove in pan of choice and in another pot boil noodles until soft
  • Season meat with itailian seasoning and tomato sauce once it is completely browned
  • Once noodles are soft grab a pyrex pan and start with a layer of noodles on the bottom
  • Then add a layer of the hamburger meat ontop of the noodles, then a layer of ricotta cheese, then a layer of shredded cheese
  • then repat last step until the pan is full or until ingredients are gone
  • place in oven covered for 15 minutes or until golden brown
