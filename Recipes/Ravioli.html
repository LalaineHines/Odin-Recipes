Ravioli

Description

This is another easy recipe for optimal flavor but fewer steps and ingredients meaning that it can become a new householed favorite without breacking the bank.

Ingredients

  • Cheese Ravioli Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce of choice
  • Shredded Cheese of choice

Steps

  1. Put ravioli into a pot of boiling water for about 15 minutes or until soft
  2. Once ravioli has soften put into a pyrex pan and pour your tomato sauce of choice into pan
  3. Top with your cheese of choice and put into oven at 425 degrees for about 10-15 mintues or until golden brown
